Larry Page

Lawrence Edward Page is an American computer scientist and entrepreneur who co-founded Google with Sergey Brin in 1998. Page served as Google's first CEO and oversaw the development of its search engine and service expansion. He stepped down as CEO of Google's parent company, Alphabet, in 2019, but remains a board member and controlling shareholder.
